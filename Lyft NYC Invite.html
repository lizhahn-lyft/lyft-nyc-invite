Lyft is hosting Professor Arun Sundararajan at our NYC Office for a talk exploring how company-led driver compensation policies can serve as viable alternatives to government mandated regulations.

Professor Sundararajan recently published a study titled "How Increased Platform Transparency Shapes Driver Choices: Evidence from a Natural Experiment on Lyft," finding that Lyft’s earnings commitment and transparency tools created a positive feedback loop for drivers and riders alike.
